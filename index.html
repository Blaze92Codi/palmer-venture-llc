FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly is an AI agent?

+

An AI agent is a purpose-built system that performs a specific task autonomously — reading and analyzing documents, annotating photos, running research, generating reports, or handling routine work — without needing you to do it manually. Unlike a chatbot you talk to, an agent works on a task and delivers a result.

What is the free glimpse?

+

You describe one challenge or task. We review it and respond with a specific written recommendation — what an AI agent built for your situation could do and how it would work. No cost, no obligation, no generic pitch. It is a real look at what is possible for your specific problem.

Do I need to be technical to use an AI agent?

+

No. We handle the design and deployment. You describe what you need the agent to do, and we build it to work in a way that fits how you already operate. Most agents are designed to be used with minimal technical knowledge.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

+

Any business where work involves documents, inspections, research, reporting, contracts, diagnostics, or repetitive daily tasks. Service businesses, trades, automotive, legal, insurance, field operations, and any owner who wants to get more done without adding headcount.

How long does it take to build an agent?

+

Scope determines timeline. Simple single-purpose agents can be designed and delivered quickly. Multi-bot collaboration systems require discovery, scoping, and a build phase. We provide a clear timeline after the initial consultation.
